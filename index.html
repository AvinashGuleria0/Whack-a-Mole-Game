🎯 How to Play Whack-a-Mole

  • 👀 Moles pop up randomly — stay alert!
  • 🖱️ Click directly on the mole to score +1 point.
  • ❌ Clicking the wrong square deducts -1 point.
  • ⏱️ You have 30 seconds per game.
  • ⚡ Moles appear faster every 2 seconds as the game progresses.
  • 🎮 Choose your difficulty before starting:
    • 🥱 Easy: Slower mole speed. Goal: 20+ points
    • 😬 Medium: Moderate mole speed. Goal: 30+ points
    • 😵 Hard: Fastest mole speed. Goal: 40+ points
  • 🏆 Reach the goal to become a Whack Master! 🔥
  • 🔁 Press Restart to try again after the game ends.
